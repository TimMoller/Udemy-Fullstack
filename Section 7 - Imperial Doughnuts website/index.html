About

Imperial doughnuts is an Independantly owned and Family run business that specialises in the creation of artisan doughnuts. Established in Early 2014, we have been blown away by the unexpected success and popularity of our carefully crafted doughnuts. Our doughnuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-riseand amongst our popular doughnuts includes: honey-glazed, jam-filled and ice-glazed doughnuts.

Currently we make 117 different types of doughnuts on a daily basis and have 2 stores in the Toronto area, one store in Montreal and one store in Chicago. Definitely pop by our locations to try one of our doughnuts, but if you can't make it - don't worry! We've recently introduced a catering service for those special occations such asweddings & birthdays and have also recently introduced a delivery service for our "Surprise Doughnut collection" box which contains 6 different doughnut flavours.
